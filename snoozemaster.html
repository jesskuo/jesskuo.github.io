timeline —

summary —

A game platform to educate graduate students about sleep health

background —
A class project from the University of Washginton's MSIM program

This project was a group assignment for IMT 540: Design Methods. Our goal was to pick a design question and follow the user design process to form a solution.

Other members of my group, Team JERRi, included fellow MSIM students Erica Adams, Renuka Dixit, and Ryan Sohn!

problem —
Lack of sleep is a critical issue.

A 2022 study conducted by Casper-Gallup discovered that one in three American adults — representing about 84 million people — describe their sleep as fair or poor. The same study also reports that the American economy loses an estimated $44.6 billion annually in unplanned absenteeism as a result of poor sleep among workers. As sleep is linked to many health outcomes and an important factor in the economy, this is a crucial problem space that needs to be addressed.

Due to the limited scope of this course, our target demographic was graduate students at the University of Washington.
